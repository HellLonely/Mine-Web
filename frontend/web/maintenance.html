Welcome to the Web Mine User's Guide

Here you will learn how Web Mine works and several tips and tricks that you can apply to your minecraft.

What does the application offer?

It adds a lot of functionalities for the management of our favorite game, Minecraft as :

Create ModPacks

You will be able to create mod packs easily and centrally, so that at any time you can change the mod pack you are using, choosing among your created mod packs.

Mods management

You will be able to see in real time your installed mods in the .minecraft/mods, with a cozy menu that offers you the possibility to delete the mod you want.

ToolBar

We put at your disposal a quick taskbar, in which you can easily access the minecraft utilities, such as the screenchots folder, shaders, logs ...

How do I get started?
